Privacy Policy for Gmail Job Tracker

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

Important: This application helps you track job applications by reading your Gmail inbox. We take your privacy seriously and only access the data necessary to provide our service.

1. Introduction

Gmail Job Tracker ("we," "our," or "the app") is a job application tracking tool that helps you organize and manage job applications from your Gmail inbox. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our service, we collect the following information:

  • Gmail Email Address: Your primary Gmail address for authentication and identification
  • Email Content: Email messages from your Gmail inbox (limited to the last 24 hours by default)
  • Extracted Job Details: Information extracted from job-related emails including:
    • Company names
    • Job titles/positions
    • Application status (submitted, interview, rejected, etc.)
    • Job links and requisition IDs
    • Email timestamps and senders
  • Usage Data: Login timestamps and session information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Email Categorization: Automatically classify emails as job applications, interview requests, rejections, or other categories
  • Job Detail Extraction: Extract and organize job application information from your emails
  • Dashboard Display: Present your job applications in an organized dashboard
  • Service Improvement: Improve our AI categorization accuracy and user experience
What We DON'T Do:
  • ❌ We never modify, delete, or send emails on your behalf
  • ❌ We never share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • ❌ We never sell your personal information
  • ❌ We never access emails beyond what's necessary for job tracking

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Data Storage:

  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) - Industry-standard secure database
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Access Control: Only you can access your data through secure authentication

Security Measures:

  • OAuth 2.0 authentication (no password storage)
  • Secure API connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited data retention

5. Third-Party Services

Our application uses the following third-party services to provide functionality:

  • Google Gmail API: For reading emails from your inbox
    • Scope: gmail.readonly (read-only access)
    • Purpose: Fetch job-related emails
    • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • AWS Bedrock (Amazon): For AI-powered email categorization and job detail extraction
    • Purpose: Process email content to identify job applications
    • Data sent: Email subject, body, and sender
    • Privacy Policy: AWS Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: For secure data storage
    • Purpose: Store extracted job application details
    • Security: Enterprise-grade PostgreSQL with encryption
    • Privacy Policy: Supabase Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

  • Active Use: We retain your job tracking data while you actively use the service
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be deleted after 12 months of inactivity
  • User-Requested Deletion: You can request data deletion at any time (see Section 8)
  • Email Content: We only process emails for extraction; full email bodies are not permanently stored

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data we have collected about you
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request complete deletion of your data
  • Revoke Access: Revoke app permissions at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Export: Request an export of your tracked job application data
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the service at any time

How to Revoke Access:

  1. Go to your Google Account Permissions
  2. Find "Gmail Job Tracker"
  3. Click "Remove Access"

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Specifically:
